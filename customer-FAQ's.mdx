## Finding Businesses

<Accordion title="How do I find nearby businesses?">
  Enable location services or use the search bar with filters (e.g., category, ratings).
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I search for specific services?">
  Yes! Use keywords like "hair salon" or "coffee" in the search bar.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How are businesses vetted on EpicMarket?">
  Businesses must meet local licensing and quality standards to join.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I suggest a business to add?">
  Yes! Submit requests via the app's "Contact Us" form.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Do businesses pay to be listed?">
  Noâ€”businesses subscribe to EpicMarket for tools, not listings.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How do I filter businesses by ratings?">
  Use the "Sort By" option in search results.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Are there customer reviews?">
  Yes! Browse ratings and reviews on each business's profile.
</Accordion>

## Ordering

<Accordion title="How do I place an order?">
  <Steps>
    <Step title="Find a business">
      Search or scan QR code
    </Step>

      View menus and services
    </Step>
    <Step title="Add to cart">
