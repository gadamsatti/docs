## Login Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Login Page">
    Visit our secure login portal at [owner.epicmarket.in/auth/login](https://owner.epicmarket.in/auth/login)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Email">
    Input your registered email address in the email field
  </Step>
  <Step title="Provide Password">
    Enter your account password, ensuring correct capitalization
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit">
    Click the "Login" button to access your dashboard
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Common Issues

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Forgot Password">
    If you can't remember your password:
    1. Click "Forgot Password" link
    2. Enter your email address
    3. Check your inbox for reset instructions
    4. Create a new secure password
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Account Locked">
    After multiple failed attempts:
    - Wait 30 minutes before trying again
    - Use password reset if needed
    - Contact support for assistance
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Security Guidelines

<Check>
  Use a strong password with:
  - At least 8 characters
  - Mix of uppercase and lowercase letters
  - Numbers and special characters
  - No personal information
</Check>

<Warning>
  Never share your login credentials or access your account on untrusted devices.
</Warning>
