# Business Information Setup Guide

Setting up your business profile is a crucial step in establishing your digital presence. Follow this comprehensive guide to ensure all your business details are entered accurately.

<Note>
  Have your business documents ready before starting this process to ensure a smooth setup experience.
</Note>

## Essential Business Details

<Steps>
  ### Business Name
  Enter your officially registered business name
  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/business-name.png" alt="Business Name Field" />
  </Frame>
  - Use your legal business name as registered
  - Double-check spelling and formatting
  - Avoid abbreviations unless part of official name

  ### Contact Information
  Set up your primary contact methods
  - **Phone**: Enter a dedicated business contact number
  - **Email**: Use your professional business email
  - **Website**: Add your business URL (if available)

  ### Physical Location
  Provide your complete business address
  ```text
  Street Address
  Building/Suite Number
  City, State/Province
  PIN Code
  ```

  ### Business Identity
  Upload your brand assets and identifiers
  <CardGroup>
    <Card title="Logo Upload" icon="image">
      - Accepted formats: PNG, JPEG
      - Max file size: 5MB
      - Recommended dimensions: 500x500px
    </Card>
    <Card title="Business Category" icon="bars">
      Select from available categories:
      - Retail
      - Services
      - Manufacturing
      - Others
    </Card>
  </CardGroup>
</Steps>

## Business Verification

<Accordion title="Required Documents">
  ### Proof Types Accepted
  Choose one of the following verification documents:

  <CardGroup>
    <Card title="GST Certificate" icon="file">
      - Valid GSTIN required
      - Clear scanned copy
    </Card>
    <Card title="Trade License" icon="id-card">
      - Current validity
      - Issued by local authority
    </Card>
    <Card title="Incorporation Certificate" icon="building">
      - Company registration number
      - ROC details
    </Card>
  </CardGroup>

  ### Document Guidelines
  <Warning>
    Ensure your uploaded documents are:
    - Clearly scanned
    - Current and valid
    - In accepted format (PDF/JPEG)
    - Under 10MB in size
  </Warning>
</Accordion>
