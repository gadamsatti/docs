## Adding a New Branch

Follow these steps to expand your business network by adding new branches.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Branches">
    Locate and click on the **Branches** section in the dashboard navigation.
  </Step>
          <Frame>
 <img src="/images/images docs/image4.3.png" alt="image4.3.png" 
         width="300"
       height="200" />
</Frame>

  <Step title="Create New Branch">
    Click the **Add Branch** button to access the branch creation form.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Form Submission

<Warning>
Review all information carefully before submission. Branch details cannot be modified for 24 hours after creation.
</Warning>

  </Step>
  <Step title="Validate Required Fields">
