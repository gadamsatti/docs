# Accessing the Epic Owner Dashboard

Get started with managing your branches and business operations through our intuitive dashboard.

<Info>
Make sure you have your registered email and password ready before proceeding.
</Info>

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Quick Access" icon="bolt">
    Login with your credentials to instantly access all dashboard features
  </Card>
  <Card title="Branch Management" icon="building">
    Add and manage multiple branches from a centralized location
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Authentication

<Steps>
  <Step title="Login to Your Account">
    Enter your registered email and password in the login form.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access Dashboard">
    Upon successful authentication, you'll be redirected to the Epic Owner Dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Adding a New Branch

Follow these steps to expand your business network by adding new branches.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Branches">
    Locate and click on the **Branches** section in the dashboard navigation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create New Branch">
    Click the **Add Branch** button to access the branch creation form.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Branch Information Form

### Required Fields

<ResponseField name="Branch Image" type="file" required>
  Upload a representative image for your branch location
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="Branch Name" type="string" required>
  Enter a distinct name for your branch (e.g., "Downtown Office")
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="Description" type="string" required>
  Provide a brief overview of the branch's purpose or services
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="Contact Details" type="object" required>
  <Expandable title="Contact Information">
    <ResponseField name="Phone" type="string" required>
      Primary contact number for the branch
    </ResponseField>
    <ResponseField name="Email" type="string" required>
      Official email address for communication
    </ResponseField>
  </Expandable>
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="Location" type="object" required>
  <Expandable title="Address Details">
    <ResponseField name="Street Address" type="string" required>
      Complete street address
    </ResponseField>
    <ResponseField name="City" type="string" required>
      City name
    </ResponseField>
    <ResponseField name="State" type="string" required>
      State or region
    </ResponseField>
    <ResponseField name="Pin Code" type="string" required>
      Postal code
    </ResponseField>
  </Expandable>
</ResponseField>

### Optional Fields

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Additional Images">
    Enhance your branch profile with supplementary visuals:
    - Interior photographs
    - Exterior views
    - Team photos
    - Event pictures

    **Upload Methods:**
    - Drag and drop files into the gallery box
    - Click to browse and select from your device
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Form Submission

<Warning>
Review all information carefully before submission. Branch details cannot be modified for 24 hours after creation.
</Warning>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Review Information">
    Double-check all entered details for accuracy
  </Step>
  <Step title="Validate Required Fields">
    Ensure all required fields are properly filled
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit">
    Click the **Add Branch** button to save your new branch
  </Step>
</Steps>
