## Getting Started with EpicMarket

Learn how to quickly get started with EpicMarket's platform, whether you're a business or customer.

### For Businesses

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="building-storefront" title="Create your business profile">
    Sign up for a business account at
    [dashboard.epicmarket.com](https://dashboard.epicmarket.com). You'll need to
    provide: - Business name and contact information - Business category and
    description - Operating hours - Location details
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="qrcode" title="Set up your QR system">
    1. Generate your unique QR code from the dashboard 2. Download and print
    your QR codes 3. Place them strategically in your business 4. Test the QR
    codes to ensure they work properly
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="store" title="Configure your storefront">
    Customize your digital storefront with: - Product/service listings - Pricing
    information - Special offers - Business photos - Customer policies
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### For Customers

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="mobile" title="Download the app">
    Get started by downloading the EpicMarket app: - [Download for
    iOS](https://apps.apple.com/epicmarket) - [Download for
    Android](https://play.google.com/store/apps/epicmarket)
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="user" title="Create your account">
    Sign up using: - Email address - Phone number - Or continue with
    Google/Apple
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="qrcode" title="Start discovering">
    1. Scan QR codes at local businesses 2. Browse available products and
    services 3. Make bookings or place orders 4. Complete secure payments
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
